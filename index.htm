Game in Peace (GIP)

A movement for a better life, starting in game.


Gaming is supposed to be a fun, friendly, and safe pastime for anyone and everyone.
No matter their age, sex, gender, sexual orientation, ethnicity, nationality, abilities, beliefs, education, or experience.
Bullying, harassment, and discrimination threaten the most basic purpose of gaming, which is to play.

Be kind to each other. Play more, fight less.
